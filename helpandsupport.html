Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Register" link on the homepage. Fill in the required information and submit the form.

How do I apply for a job?

To apply for a job, log in to your account and navigate to the "Vacancy" section. Browse through the available jobs and click on the "Apply" button for the desired job. Fill in the application form and submit it.
